PART 1 - GETTING STARTED

A. Make a new app.css under the styles folder
B. Add an alias in webpack linking to the file.
C. Put style!css!(alias) in a require statement in the main app.jsx.

PART 2 - ADDING SASS
A. npm install sass-loader and node-sass
B. Change all your extensions to .scss in webpack config and in the directories
C. Make sure you add a !sass in your require statements in app.jsx
